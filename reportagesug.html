sydney university gymnastics club

The Sydney University Gymnastics Club is a sporting club that provides space and training to recreational gymnasts.

Over the years, new generations of members have brought new skills and talents that have shifted the club's interests from being predominantly focused on gymnastics to a range of artistic disciplines including acrobatics and circus arts. The change continues to feed the club's evolution: attracting individuals from different backgrounds who take in what the club has to offer while at the same time, inspiring new interests and ways of training. In this way, the club has become a unique hub of talented individuals from a diverse cross section of society.
