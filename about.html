shu yeung

photographer

Shu has always been interested in stories and story telling. Growing up these stories lived through crayons, colored pencils, pens, printed words and tall tales until Shu saw the world through a viewfinder.

For the past three years, Shu has cultivated a passion for photojournalism and reportage whilst becoming sought after commercially for his fresh approach. He has an interest in movement, understated heroes, alter-egos and humanity midst adversity. Shu is currently working on a number of projects that cover sports, contemporary issues, culture and daily life.

Among a shortlist of achievements, he has received kudos in the Sydney Morning Herald's Photos1440 Challenge and the National Geographic World Nomad Travel Scholarship competitions.

Shu is currently based in Sydney, Australia.
