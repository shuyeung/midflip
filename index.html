Circa is among Australia's most cutting-edge contemporary circus companies with acclaimed shows touring internationally.

Their show 'Opus'is a collaborative work that combines physical theatre and acrobatics with Shostakovich's dark and complex scores performed by France's renowned Debussy String Quartet.

'Opus' was performed as part of the Melbourne Festival at the Melbourne State Theatre in October. The selection of photos in frame this month are part of a project exploring the lives of performing artists.
