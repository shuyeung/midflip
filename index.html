Unable to move from the neck down due to multiple sclerosis (MS), Loredana relies heavily on her carers, who have become an almost constant necessity. The costs of carers are subsidised by the government, however, the amount of contact hours paid for by the government are far less than what patients like Loredana need.

Although MS continues to deteriorate Loredana's physical condition, it does nothing to erode her intelligence or outspoken personality: indeed, she feels many people make an incorrect correlation between her physical and mental state. Loredana is passionate about raising awareness regarding healthcare and MS even though the physical effort has become increasing trying.

Her greatest fear is of being transported to a nursing home where in a fully paralysed state, she is imprisoned in her own body without nearly the amount or quality of care that she needs and bereft of the right or ability to be euthanised.
