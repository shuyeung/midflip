reportage

coconut products limited fire

On 4 June 2013 a fire reportedly triggered by welding sparks from work on an oil pipe consumed the Coconut Products Limited (CPL) factory in Rabaul, Papua New Guinea at around 2pm.

Firemen arrived from Kokopo and struggled to contain the fire for the remainder of the afternoon assisted by factory staff. Police and armed guards secured the site.

CPL produces coconut oil from copra (dried coconut) purchased from local harvesters and is one of the major local commodities. The factory was shutdown for maintenance during the fire. One fireman was injured while fighting the fire at the northern end of the factory.
